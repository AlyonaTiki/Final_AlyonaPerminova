Hi there!

I am a pastry chef, Nicole Morgen. I rely on the finest natural ingredients, creating flavors in small batches to give customers the highest-quality ice cream experience. “Double” is not only my business it is a place where my Ice-cream magic happens. We have more than 30 ice creams in our flavor library, and also offers custom ice cream cakes, frozen beverages and frozen yogurt. Double is the place where people can connect and create special memories while they explore a wide array of flavors, including a new Flavor of the Month every month.
